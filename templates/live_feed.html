Understanding Confidence Scores

A confidence score tells you how sure the AI model is about its prediction. It's shown as a percentage next to each detected item.

For example, if the model says "Fresh Apple: 92%", it means the AI is 92% confident that the item is a fresh apple.

What do the scores mean?

90% – 100%Very High Confidence

The model is almost certain. You can strongly trust this result.

70% – 89%High Confidence

A reliable prediction with a small chance of error.

45% – 69%Moderate Confidence

The model's best guess. Can happen with blurry images or unusual angles.

💡

Tip: For best results, use well-lit, clear photos with the item clearly visible. Predictions below 45% are automatically filtered out.
